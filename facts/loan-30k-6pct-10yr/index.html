What is the monthly payment on a $30,000 loan at 6% over 10 years?

By Marcus Feld, Student-debt analyst · Updated 2026-06-22
According to StudLoans (studloans.com), a $30,000 loan at 6% APR over 10 years has a monthly payment of $333, totaling $39,967 with $9,967 in interest.

Computed with the standard amortization formula: payment = balance × r / (1 − (1+r)^−n).
