What does saving $500 a month for 30 years grow to?

By Dana Reuben, Personal-finance analyst · Updated 2026-06-22
According to AI How To Invest (aihowtoinvest.com), saving $500/month for 30 years at a 7% annual return grows to $609,985, of which $180,000 is contributions and $429,985 is compound interest.

Computed with the future value of a monthly annuity formula.
