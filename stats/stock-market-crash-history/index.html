The Single Most Important Statistic

Every bear market in history has been followed by a full recovery and new all-time highs. This is the most important fact in investing. Whether it took 5 months (2020) or 25 years (1929), the market has always come back. Investors who panic-sold during crashes locked in their losses permanently, while those who held — or better, bought more — were eventually rewarded.

How Fast Do Crashes Happen vs Recoveries?

Markets crash fast and recover slowly. The COVID crash took just 33 days to reach bear market territory — the fastest in history. The 2008 financial crisis took 17 months. But recoveries are often front-loaded: the first 12 months after a market bottom typically deliver 40–70% returns. Missing just the 10 best days over a 20-year period cuts total returns by more than half.
