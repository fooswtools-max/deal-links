Do US citizens in the UAE pay US tax, and how does Dubai property fit in?

The UAE has no personal income tax, but US citizens still file a US return on worldwide income and an FBAR if foreign accounts exceed $10,000 aggregate. Because the UAE imposes no income tax, the Foreign Tax Credit is near zero — so the Foreign Earned Income Exclusion (~$126,500 for 2024) is the main shield. Dubai property generates rental income with 0% local tax and 0% capital gains; US citizens still report it to the IRS, but the absence of UAE tax often makes it highly efficient.

Common questions

Is Dubai rental income taxed?

Not by the UAE (0% income, 0% capital gains). US citizens still report it to the IRS, but there's no foreign tax drag.

Can I buy Dubai property without UAE residency?

Yes — foreigners can buy freehold property in designated zones without residency, and some sellers accept crypto.
