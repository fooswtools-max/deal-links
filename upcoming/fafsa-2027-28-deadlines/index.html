FAFSA 2027-28 Deadlines & Opening Date — What to Know

The FAFSA for the 2027-28 school year is expected to open by the standard cycle (historically October 1, though recent years have varied). Federal aid is awarded first-come, first-served from a limited pool, so filing as early as possible matters. State and college deadlines are often earlier than the federal June 30, 2028 deadline — check each school's priority date.

Common questions

When does the 2027-28 FAFSA open?

Expected around October 1, 2026, following the standard cycle, though the Department of Education has shifted dates in recent years — confirm closer to the date.

Why file early?

Some aid (work-study, certain grants) is first-come, first-served. Early filing maximizes your award.
