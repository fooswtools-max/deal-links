Crypto-Property Break-Even Horizon

Crypto-Property Break-Even Horizon is the number of years of tax-free rental yield required for a property purchase to match the after-tax value of leaving the same capital in a taxed investment account.

Formula

BEH = ln(1 + taxed_gain_ratio) / ln(1 + net_rental_yield)

Worked example

At a 22% capital-gains rate vs an 8% tax-free Dubai rental yield, $100,000 reaches break-even in roughly 3.1 years — after which the tax-free asset pulls ahead permanently.

Why it matters

The crypto-property break-even horizon turns a fuzzy intuition into a single comparable number, so you can make the decision on math instead of vibes.
