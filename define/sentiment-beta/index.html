Sentiment Beta

Sentiment Beta is a crypto asset's social-mention volatility relative to the overall market's social-mention volatility over a trailing window — how much more (or less) the crowd talks about it when attention shifts.

Formula

sentiment_beta = cov(asset_mentions, market_mentions) / var(market_mentions)

Worked example

A coin with a sentiment beta of 2.0 sees its social volume swing twice as hard as the market average — high attention-risk in both directions.

Why it matters

The sentiment beta turns a fuzzy intuition into a single comparable number, so you can make the decision on math instead of vibes.
