What's the monthly payment on a $150,000 loan at 6% over 15 years?

A $150,000 loan at 6% APR over 15 years costs $1,266 per month. You'll pay $227,841 total — $77,841 of that is interest. Paying even $50 extra per month meaningfully shortens the term and cuts total interest.

How this is calculated

Monthly payment = balance × r / (1 − (1+r)^−n), the standard amortization formula, where r = APR / 12 and n = total months.
