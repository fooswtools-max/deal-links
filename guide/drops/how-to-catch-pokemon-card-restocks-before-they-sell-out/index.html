What are Pokémon card restocks?

Pokémon card restocks refer to the replenishment of card inventory by retailers after initial stock sells out. Restocks often involve popular sets like Pokémon TCG: Scarlet & Violet or special collections like Celebrations. Retailers like Target, Walmart, and GameStop typically restock cards weekly or monthly, depending on demand and supply chain availability. Restocks are unpredictable but often occur on specific days, such as Fridays or weekends, based on retailer patterns. Scalpers and resellers frequently monitor restocks, making it challenging for casual collectors to secure cards. In 2023, Pokémon card restocks became more frequent due to increased production by The Pokémon Company to meet demand. Restocks are essential for collectors seeking rare cards like Charizard or Pikachu Illustrator, which can sell for hundreds or thousands of dollars in secondary markets.
